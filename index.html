DETERMINE YOUR HAIR TYPE

By Aubri Strutz

Hey there, lovelies! Pull up a chair, grab your favorite mug of tea, and let's talk hair! As a busy mom with a passion for all things hair-related, I've spent countless hours experimenting, learning, and loving every strand on my head. And you know what? I wouldn't have it any other way! There's something truly magical about the journey to discovering your unique hair type and learning to embrace it with open arms. So, whether you're a fellow mom juggling a million things at once or just someone curious about their hair journey, I'm here to share my knowledge, my experiences, and a whole lot of love for those luscious locks of yours. Are you ready? Let's dive in!
