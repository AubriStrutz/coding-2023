STRAIGHT HAIR TYPE 1

Oh, sweet straight hair, you're like the calm in the storm, the steady beat of a drum that keeps us grounded. Picture a sleek, straight ribbon gently cascading down your back, catching the light and shimmering like spun silk. It's the kind of hair that looks effortlessly chic whether you're running errands or attending a fancy soirée. But don't let that simplicity fool you—straight hair is anything but boring! With a little creativity and the right styling tools, you can transform those silky strands into a myriad of looks, from sleek and polished to tousled and textured. So go ahead, rock that classic straight style or experiment with subtle waves and curls. The world is your runway, darling, and your straight hair is ready to shine!
